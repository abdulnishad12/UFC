The Company

United Freight Carriers (UFC) is a full service dry cargo vessel operator and logistics service provider.
UFC is part of the Golden Ocean Group (listed in Norway and Singapore).

The Strategy

Being part of the one of world’s largest ship owning/operating groups ensures access to a wide range of vessels and having established relationships with third party service providers, allows us to create seamless and job specific logistics solutions for our Customers.

The Team

Our experienced team of dedicated professionals stand ready to assist our business partners in planning and executing their logistics requirements.

Management


The corporate management functions of UFC are handled through Golden Ocean Management AS in Oslo, Norway.

The day to day management functions of the company including Chartering and Operations are handled by representative offices in Singapore, Dubai and New York in conjunction with the office in Oslo.

Our geographically well placed management teams, along with a network of agents in strategic locations, allow us to provide around the clock service to our diverse customer base no matter where they are located.

Services

United Freight Carriers (UFC) is a full service dry cargo vessel operator and logistics service provider. UFC operates in the sub panamax sector with special emphasis on handy size bulk carriers and multipurpose Vessels.

In addition to expertly carrying bulk and bagged commodities in the most cost effective and expeditious way, we can arrange for bulk discharge, bagging and inland deliveries of a wide range of commodities as well as complex industrial projects. Our experienced team of dedicated professionals stands ready to assist our business partners in planning and executing their logistics requirements.

The Chartering team works with our Clients to fully understand their requirements and to provide the specific services they require.

The Operations team helps our Clients in planning and executing the operations and in optimizing the process.

The team of Port Captains attends load ports, discharge ports and inland destinations to ensure our Clients’ valuable cargo is always well cared for.
